Ship Classification:


Since ships are the most popular means of transportation and warfare in seas and oceans, they need to be classified by autonomous systems. These autonomous systems have the potential to find widespread use in sea and ocean waters, necessitating a reliable classification of their surrounding. Thus, this model helps classify different ship images into 10 categories: aircraft carriers, bulkers, cruise ships, fire-fighting vessels, fishing vessels, inland dry cargo vessels, restaurant ships, motor yachts, drilling rigs, and submarines.
